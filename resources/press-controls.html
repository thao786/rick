Press and related controls

Certified by Bosch Rexroth as having Integrator status, we are familiar with the latest in "Shaftless" technology.

NRG Innovative Solutions provides electrical control systems for presses, auxiliaries and bindery systems, with the retrofit Printing Press and Binery equipment with new controls.

Design and Build

...

We design and build control panels and consoles for press installations or for retrofit to existing installations. We program PLC controls, user interfaces and motor drives. NRG Innovative Solutions also reprograms existing systems to accommodate changes to press or auxiliaries.




Software and Hardware

We have experience with “shaftless” operation of press units and auxiliaries, most notably using Indramat hardware and software.

Our press and bindery systems are built around Allen-Bradley, Omron or Eurotherm hardware. Our user interfaces are based on CITECT, Rockwell Automation’s RSView or custom coded Visual Basic applications.



Documentation

NRG Innovative Solutions supplies complete electrical schematics in AutoCAD format.

User manuals are supplied in PDF format or as HTML documents that can be made accessible to users on you company intranet.
