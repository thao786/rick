Press and related controls

Certified by Bosch Rexroth as having Integrator status, we are familiar with the latest in "Shaftless" technology.

NRG Innovative Solutions Corp. provides electrical control systems for presses, auxiliaries and bindery systems. In addition to retrofitting bindery equipment with new controls we have also provided complete press control retrofits on the following 8 unit web-offset printing presses: N966 Harris, M1000B Harris, M1000BE Harris, G14 Baker Perkins.

Design and Build

...

We design and build control panels and consoles for press installations or for retrofit to existing installations. We program PLC controls, user interfaces and motor drives. NRG Innovative Solutions Corp. also reprograms existing systems to accommodate changes to press or auxiliaries.




Software and Hardware

We have experience with “shaftless” operation of press units and auxiliaries, most notably using Indramat hardware and software.

Our press and bindery systems are built around Allen-Bradley, Omron or Eurotherm hardware. Our user interfaces are based on CITECT, Rockwell Automation’s RSView or custom coded Visual Basic applications.



Documentation

NRG Innovative Solutions Corp. supplies complete electrical schematics in AutoCAD format.

User manuals are supplied in PDF format or as HTML documents that can be made accessible to users on you company intranet.
